What it is

A purpose-built operator interface for live basis trades.

This is not a paper-trading dashboard. It is a local-first control surface around a real Python strategy that signs transactions, waits for GMX keeper fills, and records exactly what it did.

What the product does

Runs a WBTC spot + GMX short-perp basis trade, entering when smoothed net funding clears your threshold and closing on weaker yields or margin stress.

Who should use it

Hands-on DeFi operators who understand GMX, want human-readable control over the trade, and care about seeing live execution instead of abstract dashboards.

What to expect

Conversational configuration, a visible running loop, explicit approval/order activity, and explorer-linked verification when real trades are placed.

Why it exists

Basis trades are attractive, but brutal to run manually.

Humans are bad at the boring parts

  • Funding rates move faster than intuition.
  • Opening spot and short legs manually creates fee/slippage friction.
  • Margin health needs constant monitoring, not occasional checking.

This agent handles the operational loop

  • Smoothed enter/exit logic with hysteresis.
  • Live config reload from disk while the loop is running.
  • Persistent recent activity with explorer links for approvals and orders.
